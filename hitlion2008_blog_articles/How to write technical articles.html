About writing techincal articles, comments or comprehension about some topics.
Whenever you write a piece of comments, or comprehensions about some topics, you
should never do it before you have not understood it fully. You should not
reference any books and you should not copy any books. You should write it in
your words describing the topics, along with easy to understand and appropriate
metaphors and well designed illustrations, charts and figures. But first of all,
you have to understand it completely and thoroughly. Like Albert Enstein, if you
cannot make a six-year old girl understood, you have not understood yet, either.
